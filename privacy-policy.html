Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-07

About this policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Hasabkey ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the Hasabkey mobile app.

Information we collect

We collect only the data required for core app functionality.

  • Audio input (microphone): Used for speech recognition and voice features.
  • Device data: Basic device information needed to run the app reliably.

How we use information

  • Provide speech and voice features inside the app.
  • Maintain app performance and reliability.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Audio is shared only with a transcription service to provide speech features.

Data retention

We do not store audio on our servers. Any processing is transient and used only to return the transcription result.

Your choices

  • You can disable microphone access in your device settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to stop all data collection.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at contact@hasab.ai.
